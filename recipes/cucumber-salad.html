Spicy Cucumber Avocado Salad

Back to homepage

Ingredients

  • 2 english cucumber - diced
  • 1 avocado - diced
  • 2 tb cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic - mashed
  • 3.4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tb olive oil
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 lime - juiced
  • 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a bowl and toss to coat well.
