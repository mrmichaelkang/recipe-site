Garlic Chicken and Zucchini Bowl

Back to homepage

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups jasmine rice, cooked
  • 3 zucchini - diced
  • 1 tb olive oil
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cupt low sodium soy sauce or tamari
  • 3 tb tomato paste
  • 3 tb rice vinegar
  • 2 tb roasted sesame oil
  • 6 garlic cloves - mashed
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper fales
  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 bunch green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tb toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Prepare rice according to pacakage instructions and set aside
  2. Preheat oven to 400F
  3. Diced zucchini into bite sized. Transfer to 1 large baking sheet. Dizzle olive oil on top, toss and add a pinch of salt. Bake 15-20 minutes, mixing half way through. Then broil for 2 minutes at the end to get some slightly crisp edges.
  4. Remove zucchini from oven and let it cool. While it is cooling, in a bowl, whisk together the maple syrup, soy sauce, tomato paste, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic and crushed red pepper flakes.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add olive oil. Once pan is hot, add ground chicken, breaking it down into tiny pieces. Once the chicken is cooked through, pour in the sauce.
  6. Cook until liquid is absorbed. Stir in green onions and sesame seeds.
  7. Remove from heat and assemble bowls wth rice at the bottom, roasted zucchini on one side and the chicken on the other side
  8. Garnish with additional green onions. Enjoy!
